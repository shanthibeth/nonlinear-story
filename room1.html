Changes

Results

Subjects overall responded more positively to this treatment. All retained basic motor functions, although subjects 5 & 11 suffered from mild tremors that affected their ability to perform tasks requiring fine muscle control such as typing. However the treatment only seemed to wipe their memories of the last 2 to 6 weeks. All subjects were able to clearly remember their friends and family and were concerned for their wellbeing. Despite this inconvenience no subject was able to remember being taken to this facility or any of the subsequent experiments and were subdued with the standard procedures.
